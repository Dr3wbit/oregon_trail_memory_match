About

Welcome to my match memory tribute to the old classic game "The Oregon Trail". My name is Andrew Harrington and what you see here is a project that heavily utilized jQuery and OOP. I learned quite a bit from this project and I plan on adding more features soon. You can contact me HERE for any questions.

The concept of the game is to match cards within a certain amount of guesses. There are several matches that will increase your timer. If you match all your cards before the timer runs out then you have made it to Oregon!

BEWARE! When you match a pair of "Ailment" cards, you will see your timer drop DRASTICALLY!!!
